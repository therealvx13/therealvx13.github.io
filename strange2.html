Guided By Core Values.

Craft

We aspire to create things that resonate with people. Passion for what we do drives every project we take. Our constant drive to be better than we were the day before is evident in our work and relationships with clients.

Culture

This company was built to make quality work with brands we love and enjoy ourselves while we’re at it. It’s easy to enjoy what you do every day when your clients are people you align with. Having a comfortable office in a great neighborhood helps too. Stop by some time.

Passion

This industry requires constant learning and excitement for the craft. As a relatively young medium, the web is constantly evolving and keeping ahead of the curve requires a love and certain insanity that isn’t for everyone. It is for us. We love this stuff and it shows.

Meet the Team Behind Flywheel.

  • Corben Nichols

    Corben has a love for thoughtful design, made with purpose and intent. He takes pride in learning the story and objectives behind each project and has had the privilege of working in a variety of industries over the years. With experience in branding, printing, packaging and web design, Corben produces lasting work that has soul.

  • Matt Higgins

    Matt has been building and designing websites and web driven software for over a decade. His skills have been built through agency experience, and the applications he’s helped develop have been used by education systems, major sports franchises, and small businesses alike. Matt’s love of music and the outdoors are only matched by his passion for problem solving and elegant code.

  • Chris Everson

    Hailing from the Pacific Northwest, Chris has been crafting web-centric software solutions for more than half of his lifetime. Chris' skills as a developer and penchant for problem solving keep him continuously pushing forward with the ever-changing technological landscape in a desire to always be one step ahead of the curve.

  • Jeff Blickenstaff

    Jeff is a native Houstonian with a passion for learning new skills and technologies. He has over 8 years of experience managing brand and digital projects and processes. If there’s a faster way to do something, he likely knows about it or he will soon. When Jeff’s not at work, he’s probably taking photos, at the gym, or in the great outdoors.
